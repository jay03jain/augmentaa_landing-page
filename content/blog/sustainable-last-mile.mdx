Last-mile delivery is where emissions and delays compound. Augmentaa approaches the problem as a systems challenge: reliable charging near demand pockets, software that keeps fleets productive, and operations that are measurable—not aspirational.

## Infrastructure that matches real routes

Captive hubs and charging parks need to sit close to markets and loading patterns, with safety and predictable turnaround times. We design deployments around how vehicles actually move in Indian cities, not generic parking layouts.

## Software that connects assets and decisions

SPARK EV and SPARK DRIVE are built to give operators a single pane for charging health and vehicle status. When energy, maintenance, and route reality live in disconnected spreadsheets, sustainability initiatives stall; integrated telemetry keeps teams honest about what is working.

## Intracity distribution as a proving ground

Our intracity EV logistics work in Jaipur is both service and feedback loop: it stress-tests hardware, policies, and partner SLAs in live conditions. That field experience flows back into how we commission chargers and support pan-India rollouts.

If you are a fleet or 3PL planning electrification, start with one corridor, instrument it end-to-end, and scale with the same standards you will need at fifty sites.
