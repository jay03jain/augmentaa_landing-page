Open Charge Point Protocol (OCPP) 2.0 gives fleet operators a modern, secure way to manage chargers from multiple vendors on one platform. For Indian B2B deployments—where sites span warehouses, hubs, and customer parking—this means fewer integration surprises and faster rollouts.

## Interoperability at scale

When you standardize on OCPP 2.0, your central system can discover chargers, push firmware policies, and collect richer diagnostics without custom adapters for every hardware SKU. That matters in India’s fast-moving supplier landscape, where mixed fleets and phased capex are the norm.

## Better data for operations

OCPP 2.0 improves how session, tariff, and alarm data is modeled. Fleet teams get cleaner reporting for utilization, energy cost, and incident response—critical when you are balancing SLAs across cities.

## Security and future readiness

Stronger security profiles and clearer device management help as networks grow from tens to hundreds of endpoints. Augmentaa builds around these standards so your charging layer stays compatible as regulations and utility programs evolve.

**Next step:** If you are evaluating hardware or software for a captive hub, ask for OCPP 2.0 roadmaps—not just box specs—so your network stays manageable for years.
